New Patient Form Algorithm

1.Go to nearby Clinic
2.Get queue number
2.Fill out New Patient Form
2.1. Fill out details for Purpose of visit
3.Wait for your queue number to be called out
4.Discuss with assigned doctor about the purpose of visit
5.Clinical tests to acquire data needed such as temp. check, blood pressure, urine sample, and physical examination for the doctor to make a diagnosis that may or may not be in relation the purpose of visit.
6.Given a doctor prescription/s for treating the confirmed condition.
6.1. Provided how often and how long the prescription/s should be taken.
7.Given follow-up dates to check if the condition is completely treated.
8.Go home.
